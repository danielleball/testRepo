About the Series

The Harry Potter series, written by J.K. Rowling, follows the life and adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. Together, they face various challenges in their quest to defeat the dark wizard Voldemort.

“It does not do to dwell on dreams and forget to live.” – Albus Dumbledore

  • Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

  • Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
  • Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

Magical Creatures

In the Harry Potter universe, you'll encounter a variety of magical creatures, including house-elves, hippogriffs, and dragons. Each creature adds depth to the magical world.
